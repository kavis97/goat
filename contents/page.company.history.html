Company history

PECS in India was established in 2007. Under PECS, we started Prathyaasa Vidyalaya as a tutoring centre for underprivileged children in the remote village of Tekulagudem, 15kms away from Mahabubabad in Telangana (previously was under Andhra Pradesh).

We employed a teacher to help around 40 children free of cost. Eventually, in 2010, we constructed a small building and started Nireekshana Vidyalaya. This school grew in numbers and popularity. In 2010, we had over 150 students and ten full-time staff members.

Unfortunately, in the same year, our Principal, Mr Vinay Paul, passed away in a tragic road accident. It took almost a year for us to find a replacement and also due to political and social issues plagued with staffing issues, we had to make some radical decisions.

We introduced the Montessori system and restarted the school under the name, “New Grace School” as a seed towards “New Grace University”.

Since 2007, when this mission started as a one-room school, we have provided excellent education to around 2000 children, out of whom about 400 children were offered free education.

We currently have about 55 children. Four of them are provided free education, including school uniform, textbooks and accessories. We have students enrolled from Kindergarten till Standard 3 (Reception to Year 2).

There are nine full-time staff members. One public relations officer, six teachers and two non-teaching staff members.

In 2019, New Grace community was established in the UK. We are under Links International Charity, who provides us with the charity umbrella.
