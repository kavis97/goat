Who are we?

We are a Christian Charity to provide excellent mainstream education to all children. Despite their social status, economic status, religious state or race, we will be serving them to grow up as responsible citizens.

Vision

“To provide an excellent education to children rooted in Christian values from the Word of God.”

more...

Mission

“Teach and Share the love of Christ in words, acts and thoughts towards everyone.”

Values – A.R.I.S.E

A - ccountability - be accountable to oneself and each other for their words and actions ensuring that the vision is for God’s glory.

R - ooted in Jesus - always rely on the word of God and guidance of the Holy Spirit

I - ntegrity - Act with integrity in all affairs. Admit mistakes, seek help when needed, ensure that we build ourselves and each other up.

S - ervant heart - Be ready to serve no matter what one’s position is in the organisation. Put the interests of others above oneself.

E - xcellent - always be excellent at everything. Display the perfection of Jesus in our lives.

Why Us

All staff members and management team ensure that they treat every child and their families with love and respect. We provide hands-on Montessori education, making learning fun. We will not burden the students with unnecessary textbooks or work. We consistently train and equip our teachers to ensure excellence is maintained in all curriculum delivery and to be real “word” role models. Highest maintenance standards will be in all our premises, including our accommodation and restaurant services. Cleanliness and quality of service will be our highlights.
